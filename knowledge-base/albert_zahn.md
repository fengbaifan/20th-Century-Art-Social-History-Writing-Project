# Albert von Zahn

**1836**–**1873** | Germany 德国

## 传记 / Biography

Director of the Weimar Museum, instigator of the famous "Holbein convention" of 1871 which set out to determine the authenticity of the two Burgomeister Meyer Madonna (Dresden and Darmstadt). To Zahn's dismay, the autograph Holbein was the one in Darmstadt and not the Saxony one.
