# Girolamo-Luigi Calvi

**1791**–**1872** | Italy 意大利

## 传记 / Biography

Author of Notizie sulla vita, 1859-1869, the first great history of Milanese art of the 14th to the 16th century; largely established the canon of early Milanese artists.
