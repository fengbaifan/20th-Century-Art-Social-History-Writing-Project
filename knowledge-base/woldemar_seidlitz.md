# Woldemar von Seidlitz

**1850**–**1922** | Germany 德国

## 传记 / Biography

Museum director at Dresden. He collaborated with Georg Gronau on Leonardo scholarship.Udo Kultermann sites Seidlitz among those Gründerzeit museum directors, along with Wilhelm Bode, Alfred Lichtwark, Justus Brinckmann, and Karl Woermann, as responsible for the formation of art history by virtue of their scholarship and interest in museum training.
